Who's text to sprach

Text analysis: The written text is analyzed to understand its structure and meaning.
Speech synthesis: The text is broken down into smaller units like phonemes (basic sounds) and words.
Audio generation: These units are combined with digital voices and pronunciations to create spoken audio.
Output: The audio is played through speakers or headphones.
Rule-based systems: Use predefined rules to generate speech, often sounding robotic.
Statistical models: Analyze large amounts of speech data to predict how words sound together, leading to more natural-sounding voices.
Deep learning models: Use powerful neural networks to learn complex representations of speech and generate highly realistic and expressive voices.
